Tool Used: Canva Description of Design:
The infographic I designed is titled "VIT BHOPAL Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic" and serves as a visual roadmap for first-year engineering students. It follows a clean, vertical layout with a professional blue and white color scheme, organized into four key 
Section 1 (Tools): Highlights the GitHub logo to emphasize the importance of version control and project collaboration for developers.
Section 2 (Safety): Uses a digital shield icon to represent cybersecurity awareness and the need for safe internet practices.
Section 3 (Professionalism): Features the LinkedIn logo to remind students to build a professional identity and network with the academic community.
Section 4 (Useful Digital Tools): Showcases the Canva logo, symbolizing the creative productivity tools available for student presentations Reflection:
The most interesting part of creating this resource was using visual hierarchy to communicate technical importance without cluttering the page with text. I found it challenging to narrow the content down to just four core sections, as digital literacy covers a wide range of topics, but I prioritized the tools most relevant to my journey as a B.Tech CSE student. This task helped me realize that as an ambassador, my job is to make complex information accessible to my peers.
